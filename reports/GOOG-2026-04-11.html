Thesis Evaluation

Bull Case (82): Gemini Ultra closes the capability gap with GPT-4. Search AI Overviews increase engagement and CPMs without materially impacting click-through rates. YouTube and Cloud sustain 25%+ growth. EPS grows to $13+. Price: $370+.

Base Case (70): Search remains dominant with modest AI monetisation upside. YouTube grows advertising revenue in line with digital ad market. Cloud maintains trajectory at 21-23% growth. EPS: $11-12. Price: $320-360.

Bear Case (52): AI Overviews disruption to search experience reduces click volumes and CPMs. Cloud competitive losses to AWS/Azure accelerate. Antitrust remedies force structural separation of Search. EPS falls to $9-10. Price: below $270.

Weighted Score: 70 — Bull 82 × 30% + Base 70 × 50% + Bear 52 × 20%

Key Risks

  1. AI Search disruption risk: If AI Overviews reduce user clicks on search results, Google's core monetisation mechanism is impaired. This is the single largest structural risk to the bull case.
  2. Antitrust regulatory risk: The US DOJ's search monopoly case has progressed to remedies phase. Structural remedies (divestiture of Android or AdWords) could materially impair the business.
  3. Cloud competitive position: AWS and Azure have meaningful scale advantages in enterprise cloud. Google Cloud's path to sustained profitability requires continued outperformance in AI workloads.
  4. Capex intensity: AI infrastructure requires enormous and ongoing capital investment. Free cash flow conversion could remain depressed relative to net income for multiple years.
  5. Advertiser concentration risk: The top 100 advertisers represent a disproportionate share of search and YouTube ad revenue. Macro slowdown or major advertiser churn has outsized impact.

Who Should Own It / Avoid It

Owners: Long-term investors seeking high-quality AI infrastructure exposure with the safety of a dominant search monopoly underwriting the transition. Core technology holding.

Avoiders: Short-term investors seeking immediate AI monetisation catalysts. Antitrust-focused investors who believe structural remedies are likely and severe.

Recommendation

BUY. BUY on any material pullback related to AI Search concerns below $285 (implies 26x base-case EPS — reasonable for the quality of the franchise).

Reduce if antitrust structural remedies are confirmed and impose genuine business separation. Monitor AI Overviews click-through rate data carefully.
