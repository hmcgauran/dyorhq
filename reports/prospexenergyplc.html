Executive Summary

Prospex is a small-cap European gas story, which means the equity case can work when production and pricing line up, but it remains vulnerable to liquidity, operational and funding shocks. At 3.40p on the latest live pull, the shares still sit firmly in speculative territory. The attraction is obvious, gas-linked cash flow optionality from a tiny base. So is the risk.

Bottom line: HOLD - SPECULATIVE. Interesting only for investors who are comfortable with small-cap energy volatility and thin margin for error.

Business Model

Prospex invests in and develops European gas and power-related assets. The equity story depends on project interests converting into dependable production, cash generation and reserve value that the market believes.

That makes it very different from a diversified energy major. This is a concentrated, small-cap operating and financing story.
