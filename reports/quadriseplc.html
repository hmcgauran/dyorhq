Executive Summary

Quadrise has spent years living in the zone where commercial promise exists, but full validation remains just over the horizon. At 2.70p, up on the day and trading on strong volume, the market is still willing to speculate on the fuel technology and licensing story. That does not make it de-risked.

Bottom line: HOLD - SPECULATIVE. Interesting technology optionality, but still too dependent on commercial conversion milestones.

Business Model

Quadrise develops fuel technologies intended to improve economics or emissions outcomes in industrial and shipping applications. Value creation depends on proving commercial use cases, signing meaningful agreements and converting pilot work into recurring revenue.

This is effectively a technology commercialisation story, not a mature operating business.
