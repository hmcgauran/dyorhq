Mission Ready Solutions is a Canadian and US dual-listed provider of pre-hospital medical training, simulation platforms, and medical supplies primarily serving military, law enforcement, and emergency responder markets. Business Model

Mission Ready Solutions operates across two segments: Training Services (in-person tactical medical training for military and law enforcement) and Product Sales (proprietary medical simulation hardware, curriculum content, and consumable medical supplies). The company's flagship product is its tactical medical training curriculum, which has been adopted by US Army, US Navy SEALs, and various federal law enforcement agencies. Revenue is heavily weighted toward government defence contracts. This creates lumpy, contract-dependent revenue that is difficult to forecast on a quarterly basis. The company has been investing heavily in expanding its product sales channel while managing training contract cycles. The key metric is contract backlog — any sustained decline in new contract awards is a significant negative signal. The competitive moat is rooted in relationships within the US defence and law enforcement community and a curriculum library built over years of operational delivery. New entrants face significant barriers: government procurement processes, security clearance requirements, and the need for operational track record in live training environments.

Recent Catalysts
