Executive Summary

GH Research is a clinical-stage neuropsychiatry company focused on psychedelic-derived therapies, so the investment case is driven by clinical signal, safety, trial design and eventual commercial practicality. The upside could be substantial if its approach proves differentiated and scalable, but it remains an early-stage biotech where evidence, not theme, has to do the heavy lifting.

Business Model

The company is developing therapies based on 5-MeO-DMT for psychiatric indications such as treatment-resistant depression. What matters is whether the clinical programme can show rapid and durable benefit with a delivery profile that physicians, regulators and payers can realistically support. The market opportunity in depression is large, but investors should treat the addressable market as theoretical until pivotal data and a viable treatment pathway are clearer.
