Key Risks

  • 1Programme cost overrun exposure: Fixed-price development contracts carry inherent risk of cost overruns, particularly in submarine and advanced aircraft programmes.
  • 2Electric Boat concentration risk: The Columbia-class submarine programme represents a huge revenue chunk and any disruption (labour, supply chain, design change) has outsized financial impact.
  • 3Gulfstream cyclicality: Large-cabin business jet demand is sensitive to economic conditions and corporate profit cycles.
  • 4Defence budget political risk: Bipartisan support for defence spending is generally strong, but periodic budget ceiling debates and sequestration risk create uncertainty.
  • 5Customer concentration: US Navy and US Army represent the majority of revenues; any shift in DoD priorities could materially affect GD's addressable market.
