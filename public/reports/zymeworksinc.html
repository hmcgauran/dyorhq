Executive Summary

Zymeworks is a clinical-stage oncology name with a real scientific platform and the usual biotech caveat: nothing matters more than data. At $26.70, with a market cap around $1.94B and still loss-making, the stock sits in the familiar zone where platform promise is investable only if you accept binary clinical risk.

Bottom line: HOLD - SPECULATIVE. Credible biotech optionality, but still fundamentally a catalyst trade.

Business Model

Zymeworks develops antibody-based therapeutics in oncology, aiming to create value through internal pipeline progress, partnerships and licensing. This is not a cash-flow business yet. It is a platform and clinical execution story.

The moat comes from scientific quality and pipeline breadth if validated in the clinic. Until then, valuation remains fragile and event-driven.
