Key Risks

  • 1AI Search disruption risk: If AI Overviews reduce user clicks on search results, Google's core monetisation mechanism is impaired. This is the single largest structural risk to the bull case.
  • 2Antitrust regulatory risk: The US DOJ's search monopoly case has progressed to remedies phase. Structural remedies (divestiture of Android or AdWords) could materially impair the business.
  • 3Cloud competitive position: AWS and Azure have meaningful scale advantages in enterprise cloud. Google Cloud's path to sustained profitability requires continued outperformance in AI workloads.
  • 4Capex intensity: AI infrastructure requires enormous and ongoing capital investment. Free cash flow conversion could remain depressed relative to net income for multiple years.
  • 5Advertiser concentration risk: The top 100 advertisers represent a disproportionate share of search and YouTube ad revenue. Macro slowdown or major advertiser churn has outsized impact.
