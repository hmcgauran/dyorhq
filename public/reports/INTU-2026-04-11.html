Thesis Evaluation

Bull Case (80): TurboTax Live adoption accelerates as AI-assisted tax preparation becomes mainstream. QuickBooks Online subscribers grow at 20%+ with AI bookkeeping features. Mailchimp monetisation improves. Credit Karma profitable. EPS grows to $19+. Price: $420+.

Base Case (67): TurboTax franchise steady with Live growing share. QuickBooks sustains mid-teens subscriber growth. Mailchimp synergies materialise gradually. Credit Karma remains a modest drag. EPS: $16-17.50. Price: $360-410.

Bear Case (48): TurboTax faces regulatory threat from IRS Direct File (free government tax preparation). QuickBooks faces intensified competition from Xero and freshbooks. Mailchimp monetisation disappoints. EPS falls to $13-14. Price: below $310.

Weighted Score: 67 — Bull 80 × 30% + Base 67 × 50% + Bear 48 × 20%

Key Risks

  1. IRS Direct File threat: The IRS launched a free direct file programme in 2024. Expanded access could structurally impair TurboTax's paid subscriber base and force pricing concessions.
  2. QuickBooks competitive intensity: Xero, FreshBooks and Wave compete aggressively in SMB accounting. Microsoft Dynamics also targets the upper end of the market.
  3. AI execution risk: Intuit's AI strategy (Assistants, Intuit AI) requires flawless implementation. Errors in AI tax preparation carry regulatory and reputational consequences.
  4. Mailchimp integration and monetisation: Mailchimp has been slower to monetise than expected. Email marketing platforms face their own competitive dynamics.
  5. Credit Karma valuation risk: Credit Karma is loss-making and sensitive to the interest rate and consumer credit quality environment.

Who Should Own It / Avoid It

Owners: Long-term investors seeking a compounding software business with a powerful annual subscription engagement model and AI transformation optionality.

Avoiders: Value investors will find the multiple demanding. Investors concerned about government competition (IRS Direct File) should limit exposure. Short-horizon investors should await better entry.

Recommendation

HOLD. BUY below $310 (implies 34x base-case EPS — reasonable for a high-quality SaaS compounding business with AI optionality).

Reduce above $400 (implies 44x+ P/E with limited upside to fair value on base case).
