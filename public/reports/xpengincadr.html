Executive Summary

XPeng is a Chinese EV maker with credible software and autonomous driving capability, but it remains trapped in a brutal domestic EV price war. At $17.80, with a ~$16.5bn market cap, the stock sits closer to the bottom of its 52-week range than the top. The core debate is simple: can XPeng translate strong product and software into durable gross margin expansion before the market loses patience?

Bottom line: OPPORTUNISTIC BUY. Interesting technology, weak industry structure, no profits. Worth watching, not chasing.

Business Model

XPeng designs and sells premium EVs in China and is increasingly positioning itself as a software-defined vehicle business. Revenue comes from vehicle sales plus smaller contributions from services and software features. The long-term bull case is not just EV volume, it is software monetisation and autonomous driving differentiation.
