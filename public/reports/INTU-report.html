Key Risks

  • 1IRS Direct File threat: The IRS launched a free direct file programme in 2024. Expanded access could structurally impair TurboTax's paid subscriber base and force pricing concessions.
  • 2QuickBooks competitive intensity: Xero, FreshBooks and Wave compete aggressively in SMB accounting. Microsoft Dynamics also targets the upper end of the market.
  • 3AI execution risk: Intuit's AI strategy (Assistants, Intuit AI) requires flawless implementation. Errors in AI tax preparation carry regulatory and reputational consequences.
  • 4Mailchimp integration and monetisation: Mailchimp has been slower to monetise than expected. Email marketing platforms face their own competitive dynamics.
  • 5Credit Karma valuation risk: Credit Karma is loss-making and sensitive to the interest rate and consumer credit quality environment.
