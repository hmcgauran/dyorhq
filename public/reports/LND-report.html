Executive Summary

Landore sits firmly in the small-cap exploration bucket where the asset story can sound better than the equity outcome. At 2.20p, essentially flat near the top of a very low trading range, the company remains a speculative resources punt rather than an investable operating business. With negative earnings and limited valuation support, this is hard to justify as anything beyond optionality.Bottom line: SELL — SPECULATIVE.
