Business Model

Ocean Power Technologies Inc develops and deploys proprietary wave energy conversion systems, branded PowerBuoy. The technology harnesses ocean wave motion to generate electricity, with potential applications across offshore, coastal, and island markets. Revenue streams are expected to include equipment sales, lease agreements for PowerBuoy units, and long-term service contracts. The company operates primarily in North American and European waters, with projects targeting defence, oil and gas, and coastal communities. As of the last reporting period, the company remains in a pre-commercial phase with no significant recurring revenue, dependent on project grants, strategic partnerships, and capital markets for funding.
