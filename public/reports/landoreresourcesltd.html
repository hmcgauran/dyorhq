Executive Summary

Landore sits firmly in the small-cap exploration bucket where the asset story can sound better than the equity outcome. At 2.20p, essentially flat near the top of a very low trading range, the company remains a speculative resources punt rather than an investable operating business. With negative earnings and limited valuation support, this is hard to justify as anything beyond optionality.

Bottom line: SELL - SPECULATIVE. Without a material catalyst or financing clarity, there are better places to take risk.

Business Model

Landore is a mineral exploration company. That means value depends on the market assigning future worth to resource potential, drilling progress, financing, and any route to monetisation. There is no operating cash flow foundation to absorb delays or market indifference.

For names like this, the equity often becomes a financing instrument for the next piece of work rather than a compounding business.
