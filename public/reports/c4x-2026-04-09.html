Business Model

C4X Discovery is a UK-based drug discovery company that uses its proprietary 4D physics-based drug design platform and taxonomy platform to identify and develop novel small-molecule candidates across oncology, inflammation, and rare diseases. The company prioritises out-licensing and partnership deals over building a wholly-owned commercial pipeline, which limits direct binary risk but also caps upside. The core value lies in the quality of the science and the terms of any partnership deals signed.
