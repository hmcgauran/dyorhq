Business Model

Eaton Vance is a diversified asset management group managing $156bn+ in assets across equities, fixed income, and alternative strategies for institutional and retail investors globally. Key strategies include value equity, floating-rate loans, and multi-asset solutions. The firm distributes primarily through financial intermediaries (wealth management platforms, independent advisers) in North America and internationally. Revenue is driven by advisory fees on AUM -- performance fees on certain alternative strategies provide additional upside. The business generates substantial free cash flow and pays a growing dividend. Geographic exposure: North America (~70%), Europe (~20%), Asia-Pacific (~10%).
