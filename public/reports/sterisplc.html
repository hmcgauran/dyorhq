Executive Summary

STERIS is a high-quality healthcare services and equipment company with a strong position in sterilisation, infection prevention and surgical support. The business benefits from recurring demand, regulatory importance and a mission-critical role in healthcare workflows. It is not the sort of company that produces dramatic headlines, but it is exactly the sort that can compound value when bought at a sensible price.

Business Model

The group serves hospitals, laboratories and medical device customers with sterilisation services, consumables, equipment and related support. Its moat is built on trust, compliance, installed base and the fact that customers cannot casually compromise on sterilisation or infection-prevention standards. The outsourced sterilisation operation is especially attractive because it embeds STERIS in customer manufacturing and regulatory processes.
