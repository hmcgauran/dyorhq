Executive Summary

Alkemy Capital Investments is a development-stage investment company focused on critical minerals and battery materials infrastructure. The core asset is the Tavasciute lithium project in Lithuania — a hard rock lithium project with a completed Definitive Feasibility Study and a binding Glencore offtake agreement covering 40% of projected production at competitive pricing. At 334.0p, the stock has recovered from distressed levels but remains fully priced as a development story pending Final Inve

Business Model

Alkemy Capital is an investment vehicle structured to develop and operate the Tavasciute lithium project in Lithuania. The model is to advance the project through financing to construction, then operation, generating revenue from long-term battery-quality lithium concentrate sales into the European market. The Glencore offtake agreement is the commercial anchor — it de-risks the revenue model significantly by providing a credible, creditworthy buyer at fixed pricing for a material portion of production. The investment case is fundamentally different from a mining equity: the value creation happens as the project moves through financing, construction, and ramp-up milestones. The listed equity is the junior financing layer — it benefits from project success but is exposed to dilution risk if equity financing is required at unfavourable prices.
