Business Model

AST develops and plans to operate a space-based cellular broadband network that connects directly to standard mobile handsets. The model is to partner with existing mobile network operators and effectively extend coverage into underserved or uncovered areas without requiring special consumer hardware.The moat, if proven, comes from spectrum relationships, satellite constellation deployment, and first-mover advantage in direct-to-device connectivity. The problem is that none of this matters if launch cadence, capital availability, or commercial quality slip.
