Executive Summary

Dow Inc is a structurally challenged diversified chemicals producer navigating a severe cyclical downturn, with the shares running ahead of fundamentals at current levels and the risk/reward skewed firmly to the downside, warranting a REDUCE.

Business Model

Dow is one of the world's largest diversified chemical companies, spun off from DowDuPont in 2019. It operates across three principal segments: Packaging & Specialty Plastics~45% of revenue; polyethylene, elastomers, parabolic antennas Industrial Intermediates & Infrastructure~30% of revenue; polyurethanes, chlor-alkali, PO Performance Materials & Coatings~25% of revenue; coatings, architectural paints, siloxanes Dow is highly exposed to global GDP growth, particularly in construction, automotive, consumer goods, and packaging. It is a significant energy consumer, with feedstock and fuel costs representing approximately 40% of production costs. The company generates significant free cash flow at the trough of the cycle through cost discipline but requires a constructive macro environment to deliver acceptable returns on capital.
