Key Risks

  1. Execution risk on backlog conversion: GE Vernova has a large installed base and ambitious service margin targets. Service profitability assumptions may prove optimistic.
  2. Data centre demand cyclicality: The AI power buildout is a genuine structural tailwind but hyperscaler capex cycles are not guaranteed to be smooth or uninterrupted.
  3. Gas turbine competitive dynamics: Siemens Energy and Mitsubishi Power compete aggressively in heavy-duty gas turbines. Pricing pressure could compress equipment margins.
  4. Regulatory and permitting risk: Grid infrastructure projects face long permitting timelines that could delay revenue recognition for the grid segment.
  5. High valuation demanding clean execution: At 56x trailing P/E, even modest execution missteps could trigger significant multiple compression.

Who Should Own It / Avoid It

Avoiders: Value and mean-reversion investors should avoid at current multiples. Short-term investors with no tolerance for volatility in expensive stocks should sit this out.
