Executive Summary

Business Model

Citigroup is a global diversified bank operating across five primary segments: Services: Treasury and trade solutions, working capital, and cash management for corporate and institutional clients. Markets: Fixed-income and equities trading, securities services, and prime brokerage — providing market access and liquidity. Banking: Investment banking, advisory, equity and debt underwriting, and strategic financing for corporate clients. US Personal Banking: Retail branch banking, credit cards, personal loans, and deposits through the US consumer franchise. Wealth Management: Private banking, investment advice, and tailored credit solutions for HNW and ultra-HNW individuals. Citigroup is in the midst of a multi-year efficiency programme under CEO Jane Fraser's leadership, targeting cost reductions and a simplified organisational structure to improve returns on capital.
