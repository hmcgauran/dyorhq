Thesis Evaluation

Bull Case (76): Lenacapavir achieves wide uptake in both prevention and treatment settings globally. Trodelvy succeeds in earlier-line breast cancer indications. HBV functional cure programmes produce Phase III data. EPS grows to $8.50+. Price: $170+.

Base Case (62): HIV franchise holds steady with Biktarvy growth offsetting Truvaya/Descovy generic pressure. Trodelvy grows but faces competitive ADC dynamics. Liver disease revenues stabilise. EPS: $7.00-7.80. Price: $140-165.

Bear Case (43): Lenacapavir uptake slower due to pricing/access barriers. Trodelvy fails in key Phase III readouts. Generic competition to liver disease drugs accelerates faster than modelled. EPS falls to $5.50-6.00. Price: below $115.

Weighted Score: 62 — Bull 76 × 30% + Base 62 × 50% + Bear 43 × 20%

Key Risks

  1. HIV franchise competitive entry: GSK/ViiV's long-acting cabotegravir directly competes with lenacapavir in prevention. Pricing and access negotiations could limit peak penetration.
  2. Trodelvy competitive dynamics: The ADC space in oncology is rapidly becoming crowded (AstraZeneca/Daiichi, Gilead's ownpipeline). Confirmatory trials and survival data may not support current trajectories.
  3. Biktarvy patent cliff: While Biktarvy is protected into the early 2030s, any future generic entry would represent a significant revenue event for the core HIV business.
  4. Liver disease revenue volatility: Revenue from HCV treatments is declining as the treated patient pool normalises. NASH/ASH programmes remain speculative.
  5. Acquisition integration and capital allocation: Gilead's $21bn Immunomedics acquisition has performed better than feared but the high acquisition price constrains management flexibility.

Who Should Own It / Avoid It

Owners: Income-oriented investors seeking a generous dividend backed by a durable HIV cash flow franchise. Thematic investors in HIV prevention and oncology ADC platforms.

Avoiders: Growth-oriented investors will find more compelling risk-reward in other healthcare names. Short-sellers on binary event risk should manage position size carefully given dividend support.
