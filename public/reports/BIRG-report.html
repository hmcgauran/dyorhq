Business Model

Bank of Ireland is Ireland's largest retail bank by assets, operating across three core divisions: retail banking ( mortgages, deposits, personal lending), corporate and commercial banking (SME lending, trade finance, treasury), and wealth and insurance (Johnston Campbell wealth management, Bank of Ireland Life). The bank is the market leader in Irish residential mortgages alongside AIB, with a loan book that skews heavily toward variable-rate and tracker products — a legacy of the pre-GFC Irish property boom. Its deposit base is primarily Irish retail and SME deposits, giving it a relatively stable but expensive funding profile as competition for deposits has intensified.
