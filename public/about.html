About DYOR HQ

AI-powered investment research. Do your own research, better.

What is this?

DYOR HQ started as a personal project. I was spending hours every week doing the same thing: scanning X, reading regulatory news service (RNS) announcements, pulling company reports, and building a view on a handful of companies I cared about. It was slow, repetitive, and challenging to stay on top of.

I wanted to see if AI could keep up with the way I actually work. Not replace the thinking, but handle the heavy lifting of collecting, summarising, and surfacing what matters so I can spend more time on the actual judgement calls.

This is the result.

What goes into a report?

Every company in the coverage list gets the same treatment:

  • X and social media - Sentiment scanning, trending themes, what's driving conversation
  • RNS announcements - Regulatory news for UK and Irish listed companies, filings, and regulatory updates
  • Company reports - Annual reports, interim results, presentations, and regulatory filings
  • Financial data - Live prices, fundamentals, valuation metrics
  • Conviction scoring - A structured view of the bull, base, and bear cases over a 12-month horizon

Why?

A pet project. I wanted to automate the research I was already doing manually and see if AI could keep up. This is me exploring the art of the possible. DYOR because that is the whole point.
