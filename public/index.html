Investment Research

AI-powered investment research. Do your own research, better.

Data Sources

  • Live prices & fundamentals — Google Sheet (watchlist management, real-time quotes, market cap, P/E, EPS, revenue, cash, debt)
  • News & events — Yahoo Finance RSS, SEC EDGAR, Investegate (RNS for UK/Irish listed names)
  • Insider activity — SEC Form 4 filings (US-listed stocks)

AI Models

  • MiniMax M2.7 — Primary model. Analysis, RNS parsing, thesis construction, financial modelling, conviction scoring
  • Ollama (gemma4) — Lightweight. Routine data pulls, operational tasks, status checks
  • Human review — Conviction scores and recommendations are AI-assisted with human oversight. Not fully automated.

Conviction Scoring

  • BUY 80+ — Clear thesis, near-term catalyst, manageable risk
  • HOLD 60–79 — Thesis intact, some execution risk ahead
  • REDUCE 40–59 — Thesis questioned, catalysts distant
  • SELL <40 — Thesis broken or binary outcome

This is research, not financial advice. Every report is a structured starting point — always do your own due diligence before making investment decisions.
