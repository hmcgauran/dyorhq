## Business Model
Gateley operates as a commercial legal services group with a differentiated model built around complementary professional services. Unlike a traditional law firm, the group bundles legal advice with professional services in tax, consulting, and specialist advisory -- creating a land-and-expand dynamic within existing corporate relationships. This model drives higher wallet share per client and improves client retention versus single-service peers. Revenue is generated across four principal areas: Corporate (M&A, private equity, venture capital), Real Estate (commercial property, development, finance), Banking and Finance, and Tax and Professional Services. The group serves mid-market UK corporates, private equity sponsors, and property companies -- a client base that is exposed to transaction volumes but benefits from long-term relationship stability. The AIM listing is itself an asset: it provides acquisition currency (shares) for bolt-on acquisitions of smaller legal and professional services firms, enabling consolidation in a fragmented market. This has been the stated growth strategy and is reflected in the group's historical acquisition activity.
