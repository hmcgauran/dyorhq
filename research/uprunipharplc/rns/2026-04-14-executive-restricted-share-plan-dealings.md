 Executive Restricted Share Plan Dealings 
 
 
 
 
 Summary by AI BETA Close X 
 
 
 Uniphar plc announced transactions by its Chief Executive Officer, Gerard Rabbette, and Chief Financial Officer, Timothy Dolphin, on April 9, 2026. Gerard Rabbette purchased 111,088 ordinary shares at €3.8629 each through the Uniphar plc Employee Benefit Trust and sold 233,068 ordinary shares at the same price, retaining an interest in 7,334,772 shares. Timothy Dolphin purchased 74,059 ordinary shares at €3.8629 each via the trust and sold 155,378 ordinary shares at €3.8629 each, holding 5,610,856 shares thereafter. These transactions occurred on Euronext Dublin and relate to the Uniphar plc Executive Restricted Share Plan.
