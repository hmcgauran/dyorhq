 Norges Bank has notified Bank of Ireland Group PLC of a change in its major holdings, crossing a 4% threshold on April 9, 2026. The bank's voting rights attached to shares have decreased from 4.64% to 3.90%, representing 37,106,493 voting rights out of a total of 951,275,753. Norges Bank is not controlled by any other entity and does not control any other undertakings holding an interest in the issuer.
