 Richard Griffiths and controlled undertakings have increased their total voting rights in Griffin Mining Limited to 21.04%, comprising 18.26% directly attached to shares and 2.78% through financial instruments, crossing a notification threshold on April 10, 2026. This increase is attributed to an acquisition of voting rights and an event changing the breakdown of voting rights due to share buybacks. Previously, their holding was 17.27%. The total number of voting rights in the issuer is 37,158,292.
