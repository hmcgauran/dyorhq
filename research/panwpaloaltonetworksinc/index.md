PANW sells network security hardware, software, and cloud-native security services across three main platforms: Network Security (next-generation firewalls, Prisma Access SASE), Cloud Security (Prisma Cloud), and Security Operations (Cortex XSIAM, XSOAR). The company is executing a platform consolidation strategy - convincing customers to replace multiple point products with a unified PANW stack. Revenue mix is approximately 45% platform subscriptions, 35% recurring subscriptions, 20% services and hardware. The transition from perpetual to subscription is ongoing, creating near-term revenue headwinds but improving the revenue quality over time. The moat is the installed base of 70,000+ enterprise customers, the largest network security deployed footprint in the world, and the most comprehensive platform for enterprise network security. Prisma Cloud and XSIAM are the fastest-growing components.
