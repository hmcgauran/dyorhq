 TomCo Energy PLC has released its final results for the year ended 30 September 2025, reporting a loss before taxation of £0.69 million, a significant improvement from the £6.34 million loss in the prior year, which was impacted by a one-off impairment charge. The company has secured £1.575 million from the redemption of its stake in Tar Sands Holdings II LLC and recently completed a £550,000 fundraise in February 2026. Key developments include a renewed partnership with Valkor, making Greenfield Energy, LLC a 50:50 joint venture, and the potential for well drilling on AC Oil's acreage by the end of 2026, subject to funding and the optimization of drilling methodologies. The company's cash reserves stood at approximately £0.4 million as of March 2026, with a projected need for an additional £0.6 million in Q3 2026 to meet working capital requirements and repay the remaining Valkor loan. Despite these efforts, a material uncertainty regarding the going concern status remains due to the need for future funding.
