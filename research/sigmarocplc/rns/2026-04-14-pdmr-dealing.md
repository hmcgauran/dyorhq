 SigmaRoc PLC announced that on April 8, 2026, the Share Incentive Plan (SIP) Trustee awarded ordinary shares to Persons Discharging Managerial Responsibilities (PDMRs). A total of 2,842 ordinary shares were purchased at 126.63 pence per share using employee salary contributions. Specifically, Chief Executive Officer Max Vermorken received 1,421 shares, increasing his total holding to 3,261,124 shares, representing 0.29% of the issued share capital. Chief Financial Officer Jan Van Beek also received 1,421 shares, bringing his total holding to 118,063 shares, or 0.01% of the issued share capital.

 Awards  under the SigmaRoc Share Incentive Plan ('SIP') to Directors 
   
 The Company announces that it received notification on 8 April 2026 from MUFG Corporate Markets Trustees (UK) Limited (the 'SIP Trustee') that the SIP Trustee awarded ordinary shares of 1 penny each in the Company ('Ordinary Shares') to PDMR's under the SigmaRoc SIP on 8 April 2026.  
   
 Under the terms of the SIP, each eligible employee can contribute from salary to purchase Partnership Shares. The SIP Trustee has purchased (using the cash contributions made by the PDMRs) a total of 2,842 Ordinary Shares at a price of 126.63 pence per share.
