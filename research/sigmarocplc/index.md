## Business Model
Sigmaroc PLC is a vertically integrated building materials group serving the UK and Republic of Ireland construction markets. The group produces and distributes a broad portfolio of construction materials including ready-mix concrete, aggregates, mortar, screed, cement, pre-cast concrete products, and asphalt. Sigmaroc operates quarries, production plants, and distribution networks across England, Wales, Scotland, and Ireland, supplying directly to major housebuilders, infrastructure projects, and independent builders' merchants. The business is regionally concentrated in England and Wales with a meaningful Irish operations footprint. Revenue is generated through the sale of manufactured and quarried construction materials, with pricing influenced by local supply-demand dynamics, raw material costs (energy, logistics), and construction activity levels. The model is capital-intensive, requiring ongoing investment in plant, quarry assets, and fleet. Customer concentration is a watch item given reliance on a finite number of major housebuilders and regional contractors. Geographic exposure is predominantly domestic UK with partial Ireland diversification.
