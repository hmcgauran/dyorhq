## Business Model
Simulations Plus Inc provides modelling and simulation software and services to the pharmaceutical industry. Its primary platform is GastroPlus, a PBPK simulation tool used by drug developers and regulatory scientists to predict drug absorption, distribution, metabolism, and excretion (ADME). The company also offers a set of data-centric services, working with pharmaceutical clients to analyse clinical trial data and support regulatory submissions. Revenue is generated through software licence fees, maintenance contracts, and service contracts. The customer base is concentrated -- a meaningful portion of revenue is derived from a small number of large pharmaceutical clients and from government/agency contracts (notably with the FDA). The business has high operating leverage given its fixed cost base of R&D and engineering staff, but revenue has been declining in recent periods as pharmaceutical clients have tightened external spending.
