 Mkango Resources Limited has successfully completed a fundraise, upsized to £12.5 million (approximately C$23.0 million) from an initial £10 million, due to strong demand and significant oversubscription.  RESULTS OF FUNDRAISE OF £12.5 million 
   
 Mkango Resources Ltd (AIM/TSX-V: MKA) (the " Company " or " Mkango ") is pleased to announce the successful completion of the Fundraise announced yesterday. The Fundraise generated strong demand, was significantly oversubscribed and as a result was upsized from gross proceeds of £10 million (approximately C$18.4 million) to £12.5 million (approximately C$23.0 million). 
 A total of 37,878,788 new Common Shares in the capital of the Company have been conditionally placed with, or subscribed for by, new and existing investors at the Placing Price of 33 pence (C$0.606375) per Common Share (the " Offer Shares ").
