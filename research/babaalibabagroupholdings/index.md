## Business Model
Alibaba operates three core businesses: Taobao/Tmall e-commerce platforms (the dominant China e-commerce marketplace), Alibaba Cloud (the leading cloud infrastructure provider in China, #2 globally), and Cainiao logistics (supply chain and logistics infrastructure). International commerce (AliExpress, Lazada, Trendyol) is a growing but loss-making growth vector. The e-commerce moat is built on network effects: merchants attract buyers, buyers attract merchants, and the resulting data advantage allows personalised recommendations and advertising that compounds the flywheel. Alibaba Cloud is the most credible domestic alternative to foreign cloud providers in China and is expanding internationally.

## Key Risks
