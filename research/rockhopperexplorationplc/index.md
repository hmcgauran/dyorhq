## Business Model
Rockhopper Exploration Plc is a UK-listed oil and gas exploration and production company focused on the Southern North Sea and surrounding basins. The company's core asset is its interest in the Sea Falcon gas discovery (Licence P2308), where Rockhopper holds a 50% working interest alongside a partner. Sea Falcon is located in shallow water off the Norfolk coast and represents the company's principal near-term development opportunity. Rockhopper has completed a 3D seismic survey over Sea Falcon and has been seeking a farm-in partner to fund the appraisal and development programme -- a process that has extended beyond initial expectations. The company also holds interests in the Pharaoh licences and PO9 block, which are at earlier stages of exploration. Rockhopper has no current producing assets generating operating cash flow. The business model requires partner capital to advance Sea Falcon, and success at that licence is the primary value driver for the next 18 to 36 months.
