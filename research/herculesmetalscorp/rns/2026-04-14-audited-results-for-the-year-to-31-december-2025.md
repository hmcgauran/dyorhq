 Big Technologies plc reported audited final results for the year ended 31 December 2025, with Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) growing 12% to £52.4 million and revenue increasing 3% to £49.7 million on a constant currency basis. Adjusted EBITDA was £24.6 million, down from £27.0 million in 2024, reflecting margin mix changes and management investments. The company ended the year with £93.4 million in cash, though this is after a £31.5 million payment post-year-end for the Buddi Litigation settlement. Significant operational highlights include 16 new US contracts, a 274% increase in Alcotags to 1,664, and the launch of the AlcoBreath product. The company also secured a new 7-year contract in Chile with expected revenues of approximately $26 million.
