## Executive Summary
Emerson Electric is a global automation and instrumentation leader undergoing a strategic portfolio transformation; its push into discrete automation and hybrid process control aligns with structural manufacturing onshoring and AI-driven factory automation demand; BUY at $143.77.

## Business Model
Emerson designs and manufactures industrial automation and control technologies. Key revenue streams: Process Control (AspenTech): Distributed control systems, SCADA, and operational management software for process industries (oil & gas, chemicals, refining, pharma). High-margin, recurring software revenue. Discrete Automation: PLCs, motors, drives, pneumatic components, and power generation for automotive, packaging, and semiconductor manufacturing. Measurement & Analytical: Flow meters, pressure transmitters, and gas analysers used in process safety and environmental monitoring. Valves & Actuators: Control valves and associated actuators for the process industries; strong installed base generates aftermarket parts and service revenue. Emerson is mid-transformation: selling its non-core climate and residential assets while acquiring high-growth discrete automation and software businesses. The goal is a purer play on industrial automation and digital transformation.
