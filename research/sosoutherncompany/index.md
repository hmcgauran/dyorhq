## Business Model
Southern Company is one of the largest regulated electric utility holding companies in the US, serving approximately 9 million customer accounts across Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi and Florida. Its principal subsidiary, Georgia Power, is the largest of the operating companies and operates under a constructive, forward-looking rate regulation framework with the Georgia Public Service Commission (Georgia PSC). Southern also operates natural gas distribution businesses and owns a competitive generation subsidiary, Southern Power, selling wholesale power. The company's capital investment programme (focused on grid modernisation, reliability and clean generation) is expected to grow the rate base at a mid-single-digit compound annual growth rate over the medium term.
