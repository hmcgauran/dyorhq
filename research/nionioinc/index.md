## Business Model
NIO designs and manufactures premium electric SUVs and sedans, competing in the RMB 300,000+ segment in China. Its primary differentiator is the battery-as-a-service model: customers purchase vehicles without the battery and subscribe to a battery rental/lease service. NIO operates over 2,000 battery swap stations in China, allowing 3-minute battery swaps. The BaaS model reduces upfront vehicle cost by approximately RMB 70,000, making NIO more affordable, but it creates ongoing battery lease costs for the customer and a capital-intensive infrastructure burden for NIO.
