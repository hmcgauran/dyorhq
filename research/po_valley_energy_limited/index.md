## Business Model
Po Valley Energy Limited is an independent upstream oil and gas company incorporated in Australia and listed on the ASX. The company's core assets are natural gas permits in the Po Valley region of northern Italy, one of Europe's most mature and well-understood hydrocarbon provinces. The business model is straightforward: explore, develop, and produce natural gas from these permits, generating revenue through gas sales into the Italian market. The company holds a 100% working interest in the Podilongo permit (development/exploration phase) and produces gas from the Sillaro concession (producing since 2010). Revenue is tied to Italian wholesale gas prices and production volumes. The Po Valley is structurally gas-prone with established infrastructure, reducing but not eliminating development risk. Po Valley operates as a sub-100-million-dollar micro-cap with limited institutional investor coverage and low average daily volume.
