 DCC plc has announced its total voting rights and capital as of 31 March 2026, stating that there are 85,424,135 ordinary shares with voting rights out of a total issued share capital of 87,609,229 ordinary shares.  Voting Rights and Capital 
   
   
 31 March 2026 
   
   
 In conformity with the FCA's Disclosure Guidance and Transparency Rules (DTR 5.6.1), DCC plc announces that as of 31 March 2026: 
   
 DCC plc's total number of Ordinary Shares with voting rights is 85,424,135. The total issued share capital is 87,609,229 Ordinary Shares of €0.25 each, of which 2,185,094 are held as Treasury Shares and therefore do not carry voting rights. 
   
 The figure of 85,424,135 should be used by shareholders as the denominator for the calculations by which they will determine if they are required to notify their interest in, or a change to their interest in, the share capital of DCC plc under the FCA's Disclosure Guidance and Transparency Rules.
