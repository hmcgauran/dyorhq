## Business Model
YouGov Plc is a global market research company operating in 55+ countries. The group's core asset is its proprietary online panel -- YouGov Circle -- which comprises over 25 million registered respondents across its global footprint. This panel underpins the full product suite: YouGov Surveys (omnibus and bespoke polling), YouGov Profiles (audience profiling), YouGov Plans (brand and campaign planning), and YouGov Analytics (media and content measurement). Revenue is derived from a mix of syndicated and custom research projects, recurring SaaS-style subscriptions, and data licensing arrangements. The model is high-margin and capital-light, with strong client retention and a growing proportion of recurring revenue. Geographic mix: UK (c.30%), North America (c.35%), Middle East and Africa (c.15%), and Asia-Pacific and Europe (c.20%).
