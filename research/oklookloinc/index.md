## Business Model
Oklo is developing advanced microreactors designed to supply reliable, low-carbon power where uptime and energy density matter. The commercial target set is attractive: data centres, industrial sites, remote applications and other environments where firm power can command premium economics. The company's strength is narrative alignment. If hyperscalers, data-centre developers or industrial power buyers start securing future dedicated nuclear capacity, Oklo is positioned to benefit. The weakness is that this still needs to move from strategic interest to deployable, financeable commercial reality.
