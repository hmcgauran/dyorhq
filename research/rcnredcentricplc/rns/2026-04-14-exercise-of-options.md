 Redcentric plc 
 ('Redcentric' or the 'Company') 
   
 Exercise of Options 
   
 Redcentric plc (AIM: RCN), a leading UK IT managed services provider, announces that it received notice of exercise ('Exercise') in relation to employee share options over 3,747 ordinary shares of 0.1p each in the Company ('Ordinary Shares') issued under the Company's Save As You Earn ('SAYE') Option Plan 2014. 
   
 To satisfy the Exercise the Company is issuing 3,747 new Ordinary Shares. Application has been made to the London Stock Exchange for the 3,747 new Ordinary Shares to be admitted to trading on AIM ('Admission') and it is expected that Admission will be effective, and dealings will commence in respect of the new Ordinary Shares at 8.00 a.m. on or around 17 April 2026. 
   
 Following Admission, the Company's issued share capital will consist of 159,320,466 Ordinary Shares, 496 of which remain held in Treasury. For reporting under the FCA's
