## Business Model
Allstate is one of the largest personal lines insurance carriers in the United States, serving approximately 16 million households. Its core business is personal property and casualty (P&C) insurance - private passenger auto insurance and homeowners insurance - distributed through agents, call centres and digital channels. Auto Insurance is the larger earnings contributor. Homeowners covers direct and indirect property losses from perils including wind, hail, fire and water damage. Allstate also operates Protection Services (mobile device protection, identity protection, warranty products) and a Run-off segment including legacy variable annuity and institutional risk business. The company is in the process of non-renewing approximately 350,000 California homeowner policies due to wildfire risk, which will reduce premium but also reduce tail-risk exposure.
