## Business Model
Comcast operates two distinct but interconnected businesses under one corporate umbrella: Cable Communications: The core cable business provides residential broadband internet, video, and voice services across the US. This segment generates highly predictable subscription recurring revenue. Broadband is the primary growth engine as cable TV cord-cutting continues. Media & Studios (NBCUniversal): Film production and distribution (Universal Pictures), theme parks (Universal Studios in Orlando, Hollywood, Japan, Beijing, and Abu Dhabi), broadcast television (NBC), and streaming (Peacock). Peacock Streaming: NBCUniversal's direct-to-consumer streaming service offering premium content including live sports (NFL, Premier League, Olympics), news, and entertainment originals. The cable business remains highly cash-generative, subsidising the losses at Peacock and funding theme park expansion. The strategic question is how long the cable cash cow can sustain the streaming investment.
