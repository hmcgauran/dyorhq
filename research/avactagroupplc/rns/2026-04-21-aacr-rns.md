## KEY NEW INFORMATION (vs what was already known from abstracts)

### Clinical Progress
- **FOCUS-01 Phase 1 (AVA6103) first patient dosed: March 2026** — confirmed active and enrolling
- **Two new indications added** to FOCUS-01: colorectal cancer (CRC) and hormone receptor-positive breast cancer (HR+ BC), based on Tempus LENS biomarker collaboration
- Clinical data readout: anticipated later this year
- NCT07454642 confirmed

### Enhertu Comparison — AI Synthetic Comparator
- Used AI to recreate AstraZeneca's published dataset as a synthetic comparator arm under similar experimental conditions
- Makes the comparison more methodologically rigorous than naive cross-study comparison
- Key claim: same FAP-high model, same drug class (exatecan vs deruxtecan), same experimental design

### FAP Expression Claim Expanded
- "robust activity in multiple PDX models with FAP levels ranging from very low to high, suggesting excellent antitumor effects even at the lowest levels of FAP expression in stromal cells only"
- This expands the addressable population — activity at low FAP is positive for commercial scope

### CEO Quote (verbatim)
> "These observations could significantly increase the probability of success with AVA6103, given both the ability of FAP-Exd to deliver more payload selectively to the tumor in the preclinical setting, and the success of Enhertu in the clinic."

> "The dual delivery mechanism of pre|CISION shows its potential to dramatically increase the therapeutic window and reduce systemic toxicities, offering improved outcomes for patients with cancer."
