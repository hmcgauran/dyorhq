## Key Findings

### vs Enhertu (Direct PK Comparison)
Three key advantages of AVA6103 vs trastuzumab deruxtecan:
1. More rapid tumour penetration and payload release
2. Tumour Cmax more than **one log higher** than Enhertu
3. **TSI (Tumor Selectivity Index) 3x higher** than Enhertu

TSI = AUCtumour / AUCplasma (higher = more tumour-selective delivery)

### PDX Model Data
- Robust and durable **complete responses** in multiple PDX models (not just growth inhibition)
- Seven PDX models tested across colorectal, pancreatic, and other solid tumours
- Activity even at low FAP expression in stromal cells

### SLFN11 Biomarker
- Tempus LENS database analysis across 7,769 breast cancer, 9,351 small cell lung, and other samples
- FAP+ / SLFN11High (teal) population = target patient subgroup
- Breast cancer: 67.8% of strong FAP expressers are also SLFN11-high
- Strong FAP / Low SLFN11 much less common (11.8%)

### Phase 1 FOCUS-01 Design
- Arm 1 (Q3W): Cervical → HR+ Breast → SCLC → Gastric → CRC → Pancreatic (15 pts each)
- Arm 2 (Q2W): same tumour types in parallel
- DL1-DL6 dose escalation, then MTD/RDE expansion
- Two new indications added: CRC and HR+ BC (April 2026 update)
