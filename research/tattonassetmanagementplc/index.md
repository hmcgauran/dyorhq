## Business Model
Tatton Asset Management PLC is a specialist UK wealth management group. Its core activity is the provision of model portfolio services (MPS) -- a range of multi-asset, risk-profiled investment solutions -- delivered predominantly through the UK's financial adviser channel. Advisers use Tatton's model portfolios as the underlying investment wrapper for their private client portfolios. Tatton also manages its own range of multi-asset funds. The business model is fee-based, generating recurring income from funds under management (FUM) with no direct product manufacturing risk and no reliance on transaction commissions. The adviser distribution model gives Tatton access to a broad client base without the cost of a direct-to-consumer proposition. Geographic exposure is exclusively UK, with all FUM invested in globally diversified underlying assets. This is a capital-light, scalable platform business with strong operating leverage as FUM grows.
