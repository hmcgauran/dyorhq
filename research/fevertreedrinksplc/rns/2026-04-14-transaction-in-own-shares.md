 Fevertree Drinks plc has announced the purchase of 127,130 ordinary shares between April 7th and April 10th, 2026, as part of its share buyback program. The volume-weighted average purchase price ranged from 769.06 pence to 817.03 pence per share, with individual transactions occurring on XLON and AIMX. The company intends to cancel these repurchased shares, which were acquired under the authority granted by shareholders at the 2025 Annual General Meeting.
