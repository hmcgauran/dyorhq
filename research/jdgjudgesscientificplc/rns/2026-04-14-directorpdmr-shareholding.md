 Judges Scientific plc announced that several persons discharging managerial responsibilities (PDMRs), including Tim Prestidge, Bradley Ormsby, Mark Lavelle, Ian Wilcock, Rik Armitage, John Dunne, and David Cicurel, purchased ordinary shares on April 7, 2026, through the Trustees of the Share Incentive Plan on the London Stock Exchange's AIM Market. The purchases involved varying volumes of shares, with prices consistently at 4280.0p per share. Specifically, Tim Prestidge, Bradley Ormsby, Mark Lavelle, and David Cicurel each purchased 4 shares, Ian Wilcock purchased 6 shares, and Rik Armitage and John Dunne also each purchased 6 shares.
