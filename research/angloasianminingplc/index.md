## Business Model
Anglo Asian Mining operates under Production Sharing Agreements (PSAs) with the Azerbaijan government, a contractual framework that gives the company the right to produce and sell gold, copper, and silver in exchange for sharing output with the state. This model provides long-term tenure over mineral rights but also means the Azerbaijani government has a direct economic interest in operational performance -- a double-edged dynamic that has generally been constructive for the company. The Gedabek mine is the core asset: a bulk open-pit and underground operation producing gold as the primary product alongside copper concentrate and silver. The Gyzylarahmet deposit, at an earlier stage of development, represents the primary growth pipeline and has the potential to extend mine life materially beyond current plans. Revenue is split between gold (approximately 70-75%), copper (15-20%), and silver (5-10%), with sales into the spot market and via offtake agreements. Azerbaijan and Turkey are the primary market outlets, with some third-party commodity trader offtake for concentrate.
