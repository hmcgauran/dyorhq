## Business Model
Mortgage Advice Bureau operates as a network and franchise partner for self-employed mortgage advisers and appointed representative firms. The group provides the regulatory infrastructure, compliance framework, training, technology platform, and lender relationships that allow advisers to operate under MAB's regulatory permissions. Advisers earn income through procuration fees on completed mortgage and protection insurance cases; MAB1 takes a share of that fee income under its network agreement structures. The model is asset-light: no balance sheet risk from mortgage book ownership, no funding risk, and no interest rate exposure on a proprietary book. Revenue is directly tied to mortgage completion volumes and average fee per case. The key operational metrics are adviser count, cases per adviser, and average fee per transaction. Revenue diversification comes from protection insurance (critical illness, life, buildings and contents) which carries higher margins than mortgage proc fees and provides a degree of income stability when mortgage markets are quiet. The group also generates referral income from conveyancing and other associated services.
