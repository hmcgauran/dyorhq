 Transaction in own shares 
   
 Science Group plc (AIM:SAG) announces that, on 9 April 2026, it purchased 5,000 of its 1 pence ordinary shares at an average price of 560 pence per share. The highest price paid per share was 560 pence and the lowest price paid per share was 560 pence. The Company purchased these shares through Panmure Liberum Limited. 
   
 The number of shares purchased represented 0.01% of the voting rights attributable to the total ordinary shares in issue prior to the purchase. The purchased shares will be held in treasury. 
   
 The total number of ordinary shares in issue (excluding treasury shares) following this announcement is 41,219,101.  Science Group holds 4,966,773 shares in treasury.
