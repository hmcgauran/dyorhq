## Business Model
Enterprise Products Partners operates one of the largest midstream energy infrastructure networks in North America. The partnership gathers, processes, stores, and transports natural gas, NGLs, crude oil, and refined products. Approximately 80% of earnings are fee-based or volume-based, providing meaningful insulation from commodity price volatility. Key revenue pillars include: NGL Pipelines & Storage ~35% of segment revenue Crude Oil Pipelines ~25% of segment revenue Natural Gas Pipelines ~20% of segment revenue Petrochemical & Refined Products ~15% of segment revenue Capitalisation/Bayou Bend CCS ~5% of segment revenue EPD distributes the majority of free cash flow to unitholders via monthly distributions. It has one of the longest distribution growth streaks in the MLP universe, though growth has moderated to low single-digits in recent years.
