## Business Model
RWS Holdings plc is a specialist provider of IP services, broadly split into two divisions: IP Services and Life Sciences. The IP Services division accounts for the majority of revenue and covers patent translation, patent filing and prosecution services, IP search and research, and renewal services -- essentially acting as an outsourced partner for corporate patent portfolios and IP law departments. The Life Sciences segment provides regulatory writing, medical translation, and clinical documentation services to pharmaceutical and biotech clients. RWS operates globally, with major delivery centres in Europe, North America, and Asia. Revenue is largely recurring by nature -- patents require annual renewals and translations must be updated -- providing some degree of annuity income visibility. The group serves blue-chip corporate clients, top-tier law firms, and some government IP offices. The market is structurally growing driven by global IP filing growth, increasing cross-border patent activity, and outsourcing of IP functions by cost-constrained corporates.
