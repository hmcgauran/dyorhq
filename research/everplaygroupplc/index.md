## Business Model
Everplay Group is a UK-headquartered digital advertising and marketing technology business operating across three core service lines. Its digital out-of-home division aggregates and sells advertising inventory across networked digital screens in high-footfall venues - retail environments, transport hubs, and urban fixtures - enabling brand advertisers to buy DOOH impressions programmatically. Its programmatic advertising platform provides agency and brand clients with automated, data-driven buying tools for digital display campaigns across open web and app-based inventory. A third leg, campaign management and data analytics, offers attribution modelling, audience segmentation, and performance reporting to demonstrate return on advertising spend. Everplay sits in the middle layer of a fragmented value chain: above independent publishers trying to monetise remnant inventory, but below the dominant sell-side platforms (SSPs) and major agency trading desks that command scale. Revenue is generated through a combination of platform fees, revenue share on media spend passing through its pipes, and managed-service contracts with media agencies. The business is therefore a mix of technology-margin revenue (platform, data) and pass-through media spend where margins are thin.
