 TR-1: Notification of Major Holdings 
 
 
 
 
 Summary by AI BETA Close X 
 
 
 Plenisfer Investments SGR SpA has notified Yellow Cake PLC of a change in its major holding, with its total voting rights falling to 6.91%, down from a previous notification of 7.23%. This change, which occurred on March 30, 2026, resulted in Plenisfer Investments SGR SpA holding 17,463,688 voting rights directly. The notification covers holdings through various SICAV funds managed by Plenisfer Investments SGR SpA, with the Destination Value Total Return fund holding 4.68% of voting rights through financial instruments.
