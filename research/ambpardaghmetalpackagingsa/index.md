## Business Model
AMBP manufactures metal beverage cans and related packaging, supplying beverage producers across multiple markets. This is a scale manufacturing business where value comes from production footprint, customer relationships, plant utilisation, raw material management and disciplined capital allocation. The strategic attraction is that beverage can demand is more durable than many other packaging categories, and metal packaging also benefits from recyclability and sustainability positioning. But it remains a capital-intensive, margin-sensitive business. That means execution and cost discipline matter more than narrative.
