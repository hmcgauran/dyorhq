## Business Model
Danaher operates as a diversified industrial conglomerate with two primary operating segments, following the separation of its Varex imaging business: Life Sciences: Provides instruments, consumables, and software for drug discovery, bioprocessing, and clinical diagnostics. Key brands include Beckman Coulter (clinical diagnostics), Pall (filtration and purification), Cytiva (bioprocessing), and Leica Microsystems (microscopy). Diagnostics & Biotechnology: The broader biotech tools franchise serves pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies across the drug development lifecycle - from discovery through to clinical manufacturing. DBS (Danaher Business System): Danaher's proprietary operational excellence methodology is embedded across all acquisitions, driving consistent margin improvement post-integration - this disciplined approach to M&A and operational leverage is the core of the Danaher investment thesis. Danaher is a serial acquirer that uses DBS to systematically improve the margins and competitive positioning of acquired businesses before deploying capital into the next platform acquisition. The model has worked extraordinarily well over 20+ years.
