## Business Model
Tamboran Resources is an exploration and development-stage oil and gas company. Its primary asset is a 100% working interest in approximately 1.8 million acres across four exploration permits in the Beetaloo Basin, NT Australia — one of the largest contiguous acreage positions in the basin. The company is focused on the dry gas window of the Beetaloo, targeting the middle Velkerri formation. The business model is early-stage: appraising discovered resources, seeking to prove commercial flow rates, and advancing toward a development decision. Tamboran has no current production and no material revenue. All activity is funded by equity capital raises and farm-in arrangements. Key assets include the Shenandoah-1 well (which encountered gas shows across multiple intervals), and the A series of wells targeting the middle Velkerri shale. TBN operates its own acreage and has engaged with Santos and Origin Energy on neighbouring Beetaloo positions, indicating basin-level commercial viability.
