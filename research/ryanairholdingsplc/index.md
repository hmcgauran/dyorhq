## Business Model
Ryanair Holdings DAC is Europe's largest low-cost airline by passenger numbers, operating a point-to-point short-haul network across 40+ countries. The group includes Ryanair (primary brand), Ryanair Sun (Poland), Ryanair UK, and Buzz (Poland). Ryanair's model is characterised by operational discipline: secondary airports, high aircraft utilisation, rapid turnaround times, a single aircraft type (Boeing 737 MAX), and ancillary revenue optimisation. The airline generates revenue from scheduled fares and ancillary charges (priority boarding, checked bags, car hire, hotel booking, travel insurance). The business is structurally profitable -- Ryanair is the only European airline that has consistently generated pre-tax profits through multiple cycles, including the COVID-19 disruption. Geographic exposure: predominantly Europe (EU routes), with some North Africa connectivity.
