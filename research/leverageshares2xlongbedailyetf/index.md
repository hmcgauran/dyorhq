## Business Model
The Leverage Shares 2X Long BE Daily ETF is an Irish-domiciled UCITS ETP that uses a total return swap structure to deliver 2x daily leverage against the Bloomberg Energy Index (BE). The index comprises large-cap energy equities -- predominantly major oil and gas producers -- weighted by market capitalisation. The fund resets its exposure daily, meaning each day's return equals exactly 2x the index's daily return. Over longer periods, this creates significant tracking error versus a simple 2x buy-and-hold of the index, a phenomenon known as volatility decay or path dependency. The product is exchange-listed and tradeable like a conventional equity, with ticker BEG on Euronext Dublin. It is a physically backed swap-based ETP issued by Leverage Shares plc, a specialist provider of leveraged and inverse products. The fund distributes no dividends -- all returns are reinvested. The management fee is embedded in the swap funding cost, typically in the range of 0.40-0.75% per annum on the unleveraged portion.
