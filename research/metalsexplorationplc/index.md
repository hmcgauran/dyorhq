## Business Model

The single material asset is the Los Domingo project, a copper-gold exploration property located in the Dominican Republic. The Dominican Republic has a mixed history with foreign mining investment. The country hosts several active mining operations, including the Pueblo Viejo gold mine (a world-class asset operated by Newmont and Barrick Gold), which provides a positive data point for large-scale mining in the jurisdiction. Metals Exploration's value creation model is entirely dependent on drilling results and resource definition at Los Domingo. This combination makes the project attractive to a broader pool of potential acquirers or development partners than a single-commodity explorer would be.
