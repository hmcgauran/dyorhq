 Transaction in Own Shares 
   
 AIB Group plc ("AIB" or the "Company") announces that from 30 March 2026 to 02 April 2026, it purchased a total of 2,930,887 of its ordinary shares of EUR 0.625 each ("Ordinary Shares") on Euronext Dublin through Goodbody Stockbrokers UC ("Goodbody"), as detailed below. The purchases form part of the Company's €1.0 billion share buyback programme announced on 4 March 2026. The repurchased shares will be cancelled.
