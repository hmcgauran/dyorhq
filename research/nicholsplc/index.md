## Business Model
Nichols plc is a UK-headquartered soft drinks manufacturer and brand owner. Its flagship brand is Vimto, a cordials and squash brand with particular strength in the Muslim consumer community in the UK and a meaningful presence across the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia. The group also owns Together (kids and family squash), Levi Roots (Caribbean-inspired RTDs and cordials), and a portfolio of own-label and licensed brands. Nichols operates a purely asset-light model: it outsources all manufacturing to third-party co-packers, giving it low fixed costs and meaningful operating leverage as volumes grow. Distribution is via UK grocers (Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda, Morrisons), convenience retailers, wholesale, and export markets. Revenue is modestly weighted toward the second half due to summer seasonality. The group's emerging markets division licenses the Vimto brand to local manufacturers and distributors across Sub-Saharan Africa and parts of Asia, generating royalty income with minimal capital requirement.
