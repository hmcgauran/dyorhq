## Business Model
Thor Explorations Ltd is a Canadian-incorporated gold mining company focused on West Africa. The primary asset is the Sabiensola gold mine in Senegal, a producing operation that generates gold revenue from oxide ore processing via heap leaching. The company also holds early-stage exploration assets in Senegal and Burkina Faso, including the Makabtoun and Kouloum projects. Thor's business model is straightforward: extract and process oxide ore, sell gold into the spot market, and use proceeds to fund exploration and development. The key variables are gold price, ore grade, and recovery rates. With no hedging programme of note, Thor is essentially a leveraged play on the spot gold price. Geographic exposure is West Africa, a region with meaningful political and operational risk versus established mining jurisdictions.
