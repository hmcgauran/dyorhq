## Business Model
Glencore Partnership Portfolio PLC is a closed-ended investment trust incorporated in the Republic of Ireland, listed on the London Stock Exchange. The company's sole material holding is a significant equity stake in Glencore plc, a FTSE 100 constituent and one of the world's largest commodity trading and mining companies. Glencore's portfolio spans copper, zinc, nickel, cobalt, coal, oil, and agricultural products, with additional earnings from its industry-leading commodity trading arm. GGP provides a listed vehicle for investors seeking direct Glencore exposure without the complexity of direct equity dealing. The investment trust structure means GGP's NAV will diverge from the Glencore share price depending on the discount/premium at which GGP itself trades. The board periodically reviews the discount and may execute buybacks or distributions to enhance shareholder value.
