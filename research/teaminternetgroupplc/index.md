## Business Model
Team Internet Group PLC operates across two primary divisions: Domain Monetisation and Digital Marketing Services. The Domain Monetisation segment owns and develops a portfolio of internet domain names -- primarily generic top-level domains (gTLDs) and select country-code domains -- which generate revenue through parking (display advertising linked to domain searches) and direct sales. This segment is exposed to trends in search behaviour and the broader digital advertising ecosystem. The Digital Marketing Services segment provides SEO content, display advertising network management, and related services to third-party clients. Revenues here are tied to client spend on digital marketing, which in turn is correlated with broader economic conditions and advertising market dynamics. The group has historically been dependent on a small number of anchor clients and traffic referrers for its domain parking revenues, creating concentration risk. Geographic exposure is weighted towards the United States, with meaningful European operations. The business is small-cap by global digital media standards, with limited analyst coverage and relatively low liquidity.
