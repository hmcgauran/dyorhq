 Directors' Dealings 
 Team Internet Group plc (AIM:TIG, OTCQX:TIGXF) ("Team Internet" or the "Company") announces that it received notification from Mr. Max Royde, Non-Executive Director, that an aggregate of 28,825 ordinary shares of 0.001 pence each in the Company ("Shares") have been transferred out of Kestrel Partners LLP ("Kestrel") discretionary management and control due to rearrangement of a client portfolio. These Shares continue to be held by the same beneficial owner. 
 Mr. Royde is a partner of, and holds a beneficial interest in, Kestrel. Mr. Royde is also a shareholder in Kestrel Opportunities and is therefore deemed to have a beneficial interest in Kestrel Opportunities' entire legal holding in the Team Internet.
