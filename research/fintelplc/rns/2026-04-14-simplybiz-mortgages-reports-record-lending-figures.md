 Simplybiz Mortgages reports continued growth in total lending and market share 
 Simplybiz Mortgages, part of Simplybiz, a subsidiary business of Fintel (AIM: FNTL), has reported record lending figures for 2025, with strong continued growth in both its total lending and overall market share. 
   
 The financial metrics, part of Fintel's FY25 results, show an increase in Simplybiz Mortgages' market share from 4.68% in 2024 to 4.74% in 2025.
