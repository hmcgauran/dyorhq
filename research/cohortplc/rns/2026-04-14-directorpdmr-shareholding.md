 a)    in order to effect a "Bed and ISA" transaction, on 9 April 2026, Andrew Thomis, a person discharging managerial responsibility (PDMR) sold 1,613 Shares at a price of £12.40 per share from his nominee account and then repurchased, into his Individual Savings Account (ISA), 1,611 Shares at a price of £12.4062 per share.
